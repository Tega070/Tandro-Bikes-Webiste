The Tandro Difference

Why Riders Choose Us

Stop settling for generic rentals and unreliable maintenance. Here's what sets Tandro apart from every other shop in Georgia.

Elite Bike Fleet

We exclusively stock Cube, Trek, Specialized, Stevens, KTM, Orbea, and Haibike. Every bike is inspected and serviced after every rental by professional mechanics.

Country-Wide Insurance

For a flat 150 GEL, get Georgia's only full-coverage guarantee. From Mestia to Kakheti — if you have a mechanical issue, we come to you with parts or a bike swap.

Inter-City Drop-Off

Pick up at our Kutaisi branch, drop off in Tbilisi — or vice versa. We are the only provider in Georgia offering seamless one-way inter-city rentals.

Safety & Tech Gold Standard

Every rental includes a full puncture kit with lock, helmet (optional), multi-tool, pump, spare tube, tire levers, and rear rack — all at no extra charge.

24/7 WhatsApp + GPX Maps

You're never alone on the trail. All customers get a direct line to our Live Support Team and access to Digital Trail Maps (GPX/Komoot) pre-loaded with Georgia's best routes.

Flexible Rental Durations

Single-day urban rides to multi-week cross-country expeditions. Transparent discount tiers for longer rentals — the more days, the lower your daily rate.
